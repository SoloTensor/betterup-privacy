Privacy Policy
Last updated: February 2026

Data We Access

Better Up reads only publicly visible information on Upwork job pages you actively visit — client history, hiring stats, job activity, and posted skills. No login credentials, private messages, financial details, or personal data are ever accessed.

Data Storage

All extracted data is stored temporarily in your browser's local memory (via chrome.storage.local) and in-memory cache only. Data is associated with the browser tab and is cleared when the tab is closed or the extension is reloaded. No data is ever written to external servers or persistent disk storage beyond what Chrome's storage API provides.

Anonymous Analytics

Better Up sends a single anonymous event ("popup opened") to Google Analytics 4 each time the popup is opened. This event is associated with a randomly generated install ID — never your name, email, Upwork account, browsing history, or any identifiable information.

Google may estimate your approximate country or city as part of their standard analytics service. See Google's Privacy Policy for details.

Permissions Explained

activeTab: Allows the extension to interact with the Upwork job page you are currently viewing when you click the extension icon.

scripting: Allows the extension to read publicly visible data from Upwork job pages to extract client information and hiring stats.

storage: Stores your preferences (theme, settings) and the user agreement acceptance status locally in your browser.

host_permissions (upwork.com/jobs/*): Limits the extension to only operate on Upwork job posting pages — it cannot access any other websites.

What We Do NOT Do

We do not collect, transmit, sell, or share any personal data. We do not track your browsing activity outside of Upwork job pages. We do not access your Upwork account credentials. We do not store data on external servers.

Your Control

You can clear all stored extension data at any time via chrome://extensions → Better Up → Details → Clear data. You can uninstall the extension at any time to completely opt out of all data collection including anonymous analytics.
