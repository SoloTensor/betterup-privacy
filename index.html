Privacy Policy

Better Up — Upwork Client Intelligence Extension

Last updated: March 2026
🔍

Data We Access

Better Up reads only publicly visible information on Upwork job pages you actively visit — client history, hiring stats, job activity, and posted skills. No login credentials, private messages, financial details, or personal data are ever accessed.

💾

Data Storage

All extracted data is stored temporarily in your browser's local memory and in-memory cache only. Data is associated with the browser tab and is cleared when the tab is closed or the extension is reloaded.

No data is ever written to external servers or persistent disk storage beyond what Chrome's storage API provides.

💬

Feedback System

When you submit feedback through the extension, your message, email, feedback type, screenshots (if any), browser info, and extension version are sent to a secure server to create a support ticket. This data is only used to address your feedback.

📊

Anonymous Analytics

Better Up receives anonymous heart beatwhen you use the extension. We never receive your name, email, Upwork account, browsing history, or any other identifiable information.

Google may estimate your approximate country or city as part of their standard analytics service. See Google's Privacy Policy for details.

🔐

Permissions Explained

activeTab Allows the extension to interact with the Upwork job page you are currently viewing when you click the extension icon.
scripting Allows the extension to read publicly visible data from Upwork job pages to extract client information and hiring stats.
storage Keeps your preferences (theme, settings) and the user agreement acceptance in your browser.
host_permissions Limits the extension to only operate on Upwork job posting pages (upwork.com/jobs/*) — it cannot access any other websites.
🚫

We Do NOT:

Collect personal data
Track your browsing
Access your Upwork login
Send any identifying data to servers
Sell or share any data
Access non-Upwork sites
⚙️

Your Control

You can clear all stored extension data at any time via chrome://extensions → Better Up → Details → Clear data.

You can uninstall the extension to completely opt out of all local data collection.
